Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Feynman Notes is a browser extension that helps users highlight webpage content, add notes, export reading notes, and sync their annotations with notes.bluetech.top.

What data we collect

  • Webpage content that the user chooses to annotate, highlight, export, or save.
  • User-provided notes, comments, and annotation content.
  • Authentication information needed to keep the user signed in to notes.bluetech.top.

How we use data

  • To display highlights and notes on webpages selected by the user.
  • To save, sync, publish, and export the user’s notes and annotations.
  • To maintain the user’s signed-in session with notes.bluetech.top.

How data is shared

User data is transmitted to notes.bluetech.top only to provide the core note-taking, syncing, and publishing features requested by the user. We do not sell user data and do not use user data for advertising or profiling.

Cookies

The extension uses cookies only to store and read the user’s authenticated session for notes.bluetech.top so the user can sign in and use syncing and publishing features.

Third parties

We do not share user data with third parties except as necessary to operate notes.bluetech.top and related extension functionality.

Data security

We use reasonable measures to protect user data during transmission and storage.
